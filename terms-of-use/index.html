2. Description of Service

KyberQ is a local-first encrypted vault application that lets you:

  • Encrypt and store files, photos, videos, and documents on your device
  • Organize content in folders, albums, and collections
  • Optionally use a separate secret (secondary) vault
  • Unlock your vault with a password, PIN, and optional biometrics
  • Use security features such as auto-lock, intruder photo capture, and secure viewing
  • Subscribe to Premium for expanded features and limits

KyberQ is not a cloud storage or remote backup service unless you choose to export or back up data to a location you control (for example, iCloud Drive, Google Drive, or local files).
