Privacy Policy — KyberQ

Effective date: June 7, 2026 Last updated: June 7, 2026

1. Introduction

KyberQ ("KyberQ," "we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application published by Arktech Pvt Ltd that lets you encrypt, store, organize, and protect files on your device in a secure vault.

This Privacy Policy explains what information KyberQ handles, how that information is used, what stays on your device, and what choices you have. It applies to the KyberQ app on iOS and Android and to related pages at kyberq.app.

By using KyberQ, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. Summary

  • Your vault files stay on your device. KyberQ does not upload, sync, or store your encrypted vault contents on our servers.
  • No sale of personal data. We do not sell your personal information.
  • Local-first account model. KyberQ does not use a cloud login service. Your email, vault password or PIN, recovery code, and encrypted files are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or back them up yourself.
  • Limited diagnostics. When enabled in production builds, the app may send operational diagnostic logs to our logging provider. These logs do not include vault file contents, passwords, PINs, recovery codes, or decrypted data.
  • Apple handles subscriptions. Premium purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card details.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information you provide

Information you provide or that is created on your device:

  • Email address — Stored on your device only. May appear in optional diagnostic logs (see Section 3.5).
  • Vault password or PIN — Stored on your device only (encrypted storage / secure keychain). Never sent to us.
  • Recovery code — Stored on your device only. Never sent to us.
  • Secret vault credentials — Stored on your device only. Never sent to us.
  • Files, photos, videos, and documents you encrypt — Stored on your device only. Never sent to us unless you explicitly export or back up.

Your email is used as a local account identifier on your device. It is not used as a remote sign-in credential and we do not operate a KyberQ cloud account tied to your email.

3.2 Vault and app data stored on your device

KyberQ stores the following locally on your phone or tablet:

  • Encrypted vault files and folders
  • Album and collection metadata
  • Optional secret (secondary) vault data, separate from your primary vault
  • App settings and preferences
  • Subscription entitlement cache (whether Premium is active on this device)
  • Intruder photos captured by the front camera after repeated failed unlock attempts (stored separately from your vault, in app-private storage)
  • Temporary working files during encrypt, decrypt, import, or export operations

We do not operate a KyberQ cloud that holds your vault contents.

3.3 Device permissions and on-device processing

KyberQ may request access to device features only when needed for features you use:

  • Face ID / Touch ID / biometrics — Optional vault unlock after you authenticate with your password or PIN. Biometric data is processed by your device operating system; KyberQ does not collect or store biometric templates.
  • Photo Library (read) — Import photos and videos into your vault when you choose to.
  • Photo Library (add/save) — Save decrypted files to your photo library when you choose "Save to phone/gallery."
  • Camera — Capture intruder photos after repeated incorrect unlock attempts, if you enable or the feature triggers. Photos remain on your device in Intruder settings.
  • Notifications — Security alerts and important vault-related notices, if you grant permission.
  • Files & documents (file picker) — Import files or vault backups that you select through the system file picker.

You can review and manage permissions in Settings → App permissions inside KyberQ or in your device system settings.

3.4 Subscription and purchase information

If you subscribe to KyberQ Premium:

  • Payment is processed by Apple through In-App Purchase (StoreKit).
  • Apple may collect payment and billing information according to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • We receive subscription status information from Apple (for example, whether a subscription is active) so we can unlock Premium features on your device.
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions at: Apple Subscriptions.

On Android, payments are processed by Google Play according to Google's Privacy Policy.

3.5 Diagnostic and operational logs (optional)

In production builds where diagnostic logging is enabled, KyberQ may send operational logs over the internet to our logging service provider (PostHog, via OpenTelemetry log export) to help us maintain reliability and security.

These logs may include:

  • App version and build number
  • Device model
  • Subscription tier (free, monthly, yearly)
  • Your locally saved email address (if present on the device)
  • Anonymized event codes (for example, vault created, backup started, encryption completed)
  • Non-content technical details such as error types or high-level operation status

These logs do not include:

  • Vault file contents
  • Decrypted file data
  • Passwords, PINs, or recovery codes
  • Intruder photo images
  • Full filenames or personal content from inside your vault

Diagnostic logging requires an internet connection. If your device is offline, logs are not sent. Logging of vault-related events occurs only after a vault exists on the device.

We use these logs for app operation, troubleshooting, abuse prevention, and product improvement — not for advertising or cross-app tracking.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

KyberQ is designed so that we do not collect or receive:

  • Your encrypted or decrypted vault files
  • Your vault password, PIN, or recovery code
  • Secret vault contents or credentials
  • Intruder snapshot images
  • Contacts, location, browsing history, or advertising identifiers (such as IDFA) for tracking
  • A centralized KyberQ account database on our servers

If you do not create a vault backup or export files yourself, your vault data remains on your device under your control.

5. How We Use Information

We use the limited information described above to:

  • Provide core app functionality (encryption, vault management, albums, secret vault, intruder protection)
  • Process Premium entitlements through Apple or Google Play
  • Remember your local preferences and unlock settings
  • Send optional security or app-related notifications (if enabled)
  • Diagnose crashes, errors, and reliability issues through operational logs
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your vault contents for advertising, profiling, or marketing.

6. Encryption and Security

KyberQ uses strong encryption, including post-quantum and hybrid cryptographic methods, to protect files at rest on your device. Credentials are protected using platform secure storage (such as the iOS Keychain and Android Keystore where applicable).

Important: If you lose your password or PIN and recovery code, we cannot recover your vault data. KyberQ is built so that we do not hold keys that would let us decrypt your files.

Security features may also include:

  • Optional biometric unlock
  • Auto-lock when the app goes to background
  • App switcher privacy (content hidden in recent apps preview)
  • Secure viewer protections on supported devices

No security method is perfect. You are responsible for safeguarding your credentials and any backup files you create.

7. Backup, Restore, Export, and Sharing

KyberQ lets you control data leaving the app:

  • Vault backup (Premium): Creates an encrypted backup file that you choose where to store (for example, Files, iCloud Drive, or another location you select).
  • Restore: Imports a backup you provide.
  • Save to gallery / export: Writes decrypted content to your photo library or other destinations only when you initiate the action.
  • Share: Shares files only when you choose to share through system share sheets.

We do not automatically upload backups to KyberQ servers.

8. Secret Vault

KyberQ offers an optional secret (secondary) vault with its own credentials and recovery data. Secret vault files and metadata are stored locally on your device, separately from your primary vault. Deleting the secret vault removes its credentials and files from your device. Deleting your main account removes all local app data, including any secret vault.

9. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share limited information only in these cases:

  • Apple / Google — Purchase and subscription status, for In-App Purchase processing and entitlement verification.
  • PostHog (logging provider) — Diagnostic logs described in Section 3.5, for app reliability and security monitoring.
  • Legal / safety — Information we are required to disclose, to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights and safety.

We require service providers to handle data only for authorized purposes and in line with this policy.

10. Data Retention

  • Vault files, credentials, recovery data, intruder photos — Until you delete them, delete your account in the app, or uninstall the app (subject to device backup behavior).
  • Local email and preferences — Until you change or delete them, or delete your account.
  • Subscription cache — Until cleared on account deletion or app removal.
  • Diagnostic logs (PostHog) — Retained according to our logging provider configuration and internal retention practices; typically for operational troubleshooting, not indefinitely for vault content (which is never logged).

When you use Delete account in Settings, KyberQ removes local vault data, credentials, intruder photos, cached subscription data, and the local database from your device.

11. Your Choices and Controls

You can:

  • Decline permissions — Some features will not work without the relevant permission (for example, import from Photos without photo access).
  • Disable biometrics — In KyberQ Settings.
  • Delete intruder photos — In Intruder settings.
  • Delete secret vault — From secret vault settings.
  • Delete your account — Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes local vault data on that device.
  • Manage subscriptions — Through your Apple ID or Google Play subscription settings.
  • Export or back up your data — Using in-app backup/export features before deleting your account.

Because vault data is stored locally, uninstalling the app without a backup may permanently remove access to your encrypted files on that device.

12. Children's Privacy

KyberQ is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete applicable diagnostic records we control.

Where applicable law grants you rights (such as access, correction, deletion, or objection), you may contact us at the address below. If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, our lawful bases for processing diagnostic data typically include legitimate interests (app security and reliability) and, where applicable, contract (providing the service you requested).
